Lab 15: AJAX

Challenge

The chalenge in this lab was to create a user interface button, and have a function tied to the button. Once the button is clicked, an jQuery AJAX call is then made to get output, which is then assigned to a div output.

Problems

One of the first few problems came with improper placements of closing parenthesis and curly brackets. After those were resolved, next came the issue of the visible output breaking page format, something we solved using a method we employed in lab 13.

Results

Once those two were addressed, our site was up and functional!
